The Playbook Heading System
# Main Title (Use Only Once)
The single hashtag # is reserved strictly for the main title of the entire document. You only use this once at the very top of your file.

Example: # Yvonne Martinez - Enterprise AI Operations Playbook

## Phases and Major Milestones (Level 2)
Use two hashtags ## for your major operational blocks. This includes your Phases, your Sync Checkpoints, and your final Troubleshooting Reminders section.

Examples: * ## Phase 1 — Project Initialization & Sandbox Setup

### Prompts, Instructions, and Terminal Steps (Level 3)
